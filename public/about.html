Hope Farm School is a boarding school for inner-city youth. Its mission is to disciple, train, and educate young men in the Spirit of Jesus Christ, all in a farm setting. Through Farmboy Industries we teach our students business principles, and make the learning real by selling our products. Proceeds support our school. Visit hopefarmschool.com!
